San Francisco

San Francisco is located in California, and is the only consolidated city-county in California as well. It is about 46.9 (121 km) square miles, and contains 18,187 people per square mile. It is the second-most densely populated major city in the United States, right after New York City.

San Francisco has many places to visit for tourism, such as the Golden Gate Bridge, Yerba Buena Gardens, and is a nice place to just explore around. An amazing fact about San Francisco is that it was home to the first lesbian-rights orginization of the United Sates.
