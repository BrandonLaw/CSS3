Las Vegas

Las Vegas is the most populated cities in the state of Nevada. The main public transportation is the RTC Transit. This is provided by Greyhound. The city is a majority of White and Non-Hispanic people, and the least amount of Asian people.

The main attractions of Las Vegas are the casinos and hotels, with the fresh smell of cigarette smoke as you walk in. With a 1% chance of winning the lottery and a 99% chance of suffocating, what's not to love. All jokes aside, it is a beautiful city and there are a ton of reasons to visit.
